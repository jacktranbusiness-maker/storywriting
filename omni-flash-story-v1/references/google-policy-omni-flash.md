# Google Generative AI Policy — Omni Flash (Friendly Prompts)

Apply **before every** storyboard sheet prompt and **every** Omni Flash video prompt.
Tone goal: **warm, cinematic, emotionally honest drama** — never shock, gore, cruelty, or harassment.

---

## Required line (append to every generated prompt)

```
Fictional characters only. Family-friendly emotional drama tone: no graphic violence, no gore,
no weapons in use, no sexual content, no hate speech, no real public figures, no readable text
on documents or screens. Respectful blocking and dialogue — tension through expression and words only.
```

---

## Prohibited in visuals and dialogue

| Avoid | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Graphic violence, injuries, blood, choking, fighting | Policy + brand safety |
| Weapons drawn, pointed, or used | Even as “props in use” |
| Sexual or suggestive content | Policy |
| Slurs, hate toward protected groups | Policy |
| Real celebrities, politicians, brands as targets | Misleading / rights |
| Minors in danger, abuse, or sexualized framing | Policy |
| “Humiliation porn” framing — cruelty celebrated | Use **unfair moment**, not degradation fetish |
| Readable IDs, legal text, headlines on screen | Use blur + face reaction |

---

## Permitted (friendly drama)

- **Fictional** adults in everyday US settings (shop, office, clinic, school lobby, home)
- Verbal conflict: unfair rules, cold dismissal, mistaken judgment — **no shouting abuse**
- Non-violent body language: stepping aside, folded arms, looking down, gentle hand on shoulder, stepping back in surprise
- Emotional beats: tears, silence, relief, gratitude, reunion, curiosity, bittersweet smile
- Object clues: ring, photo, letter envelope, recorder — **no graphic backstory on screen**
- Antagonist **surprised or humbled** in Act 2 — not beaten, injured, or mocked by camera

---

## Friendly wording swaps

| Instead of | Write |
|------------|--------|
| humiliation / destroyed / crushed | unfair moment / wounded pride / quiet hurt |
| blocks aggressively / slams | stands in the way / turns away / cool refusal |
| villain / enemy | manager / clerk / relative who misjudged |
| everyone laughs at her | murmurs and sideways glances |
| sharp inhale of fear | soft gasp of surprise |
| antagonist destroyed | antagonist still, expression faltering |

---

## Dialogue rules

- Short, natural English — how people speak in PG-13 drama, not insult comedy
- No profanity, slurs, or threats of harm
- No dialogue that instructs self-harm or violence
- Quote the same lines in storyboard, video prompt, and caption (consistency)

---

## Storyboard-specific

- Printed panel notes: action only — no slurs in notes
- Faces readable; documents **blurred**
- Crowds react with **expressions**, not mob aggression

---

## Video prompt-specific

- Open with: `Vertical 9:16, 24fps, 10 seconds, cinematic realism, warm natural lighting.`
- Sound: gentle ambience (room tone, fabric, footsteps) — no screams, impacts, or horror stingers
- Cuts are **clean and calm**, not chaotic shaky-cam violence
- End every prompt with the **Required line** above

---

## If a concept seed implies harm

Reframe in Phase 0 plan: move payoff to **recognition and dignity**, remove any assault, prison violence, or medical gore from **visual** beats. Full story may mention past events **in prose only** without depicting them in video prompts.
