# Act Storyboard Sheet Reference

Read `google-policy-omni-flash.md` first.

> **Omni-Flash-Story v1:** each story uses **exactly 2 acts** (20s total). See `two-act-by-engine.md` for engine beats (E00–E22).

One sheet per act. Each act = 10 seconds = 4 panels.

---

## Specs

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Sheet | 16:9 widescreen |
| Panels | 4 equal vertical 9:16, side-by-side, full height |
| Timeline | 0–3s / 3–6s / 6–8s / 8–10s |
| Notes | One short line under each panel — `F1 0-3s | action | "dialogue"` |
| Style | Cinematic realism, warm natural lighting, 35mm lens look |
| Setting | US location — same lighting across all 4 panels in this act |

## Continuity between acts

**Act 1 Frame 1:** Establish scene, characters, warm lighting.

**Act 2 Frame 1:** Must open with:

```
Continue from Act 1 Frame 4 — [who is where, same outfits, same lighting]. [New action begins]
```

---

## Panel roles — 2-act structure only

### Act 1 — Hook + Build (0–10s)
- F1: Unfair moment or mystery hook (engine-appropriate) — calm framing
- F2: Emotional reaction or object tease
- F3: Pressure deepens — witnesses or quiet escalation
- F4: End on **withhold** — do not show full payoff

### Act 2 — Turn + Tease (10–20s)
- F1: Continuation from Act 1 F4
- F2: Turn begins — recognition line, kindness returned, or explanation starting
- F3: Faces and gestures — respect, surprise, relief (engine card)
- F4: Wide or close — **tease** resolution; misjudging character may look humbled in background — not harmed

For engines **without antagonist**, Act 2 F4 = emotional payoff tease (warmth, belonging, tears of relief).

---

## Rules

- One English dialogue line per panel — PG-13, no slurs or threats
- Action tags: `[Manager gestures toward exit]` not aggressive violence
- No readable text on surfaces — blur documents
- Printed notes stay short
- Read per-item **engine** in `story-engines.md` — not every story is E00 humiliation
