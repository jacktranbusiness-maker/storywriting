## Photo rule

All engines use **cinematic** photo rule unless noted:

- Show scroll-stop emotional peak
- Partial twist visible OK (see `nano-banana-2.md`)
- Full resolution (signed deed, court judgment text, stock transfer) stays out of frame

**Action-in-photo** (show peak action): rare in this pipeline — F06 CEO walk to break room, F08 scar reveal moment (shoulder only, not folder contents).
