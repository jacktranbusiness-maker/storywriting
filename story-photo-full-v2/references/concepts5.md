## Family F01 — Wedding Ballroom Public Meltdown

Public wedding/reception scenes where humiliation stacks in front of guests, cameras, and family — multiple reveals in one room.

1. **The Father's Buried Secret**: Groom begs his father to speak at the mic → bride reveals the groom's father was engaged to her first
2. **The Paternity Mic Drop**: Best man toasts the happy couple → bride places a hand on her stomach and says the child isn't only his
3. **The Witness Phones**: Guests stop dancing and start recording → the maid of honor reads texts proving the groom booked a second venue for someone else
4. **The Stepfather's Envelope**: Father of the bride refuses to walk her down the aisle → a sealed envelope from her late mother names the "stepfather" as her real father
5. **The DJ's Wrong Song**: The first dance starts → the DJ plays a voicemail the bride left for another man last week
6. **The Cake Message**: Staff wheel in the wedding cake → blue icing spells out a mistress's name instead of the bride's
7. **The Ring Box Empty**: Groom opens the ring box for vows → inside is a divorce petition already signed by his parents
8. **The Live Stream Comment**: Someone projects the reception live → a comment from an unknown account says "check the hotel suite now"
9. **The Bridesmaid's Ultrasound**: Toast time → bridesmaid stands and shows an ultrasound dated during the engagement
10. **The Father-Daughter Dance Cut**: Father pulls bride aside mid-dance → whispers that the groom's family laundered money through her father's company
11. **The Officiant Stops**: Minister raises a hand before "I do" → court order forbids the marriage until a paternity test is filed
12. **The Champagne Poison Joke**: Groom's mother laughs about "handling the bride" → caterer announces the bride's glass was switched by mistake — and who ordered it
13. **The Missing Groomsmen**: Two groomsmen vanish during photos → they return with a woman who claims the groom proposed to her yesterday
14. **The Video Montage Twist**: Childhood slideshow plays → footage shows the bride with the groom's brother, not him
15. **The Seating Chart War**: Ushers seat the wrong family → the "excluded" table stands: three children who call the bride "Mom"
16. **The Father's Second Wife**: Groom's father finally speaks into the mic → introduces a woman the family thought died twenty years ago
17. **The Pre-Nup on the Screen**: AV tech "accidentally" mirrors a laptop → guests see a pre-nup giving everything to the groom's mother
18. **The Bouquet Catch**: Bride throws the bouquet → the woman who catches it is the one the groom's sister warned her about
19. **The Band's Last Song**: Band plays the couple's song → lyrics were rewritten overnight to describe an affair
20. **The Security at the Door**: Bouncers block a late arrival → it's the bride's first husband, still legally married in another state
21. **The Toast That Won't End**: Best man's toast turns cruel → the bride takes the mic calm and lists every loan she secretly paid off for the groom
22. **The Photographer's Receipt**: Photographer interrupts the kiss → invoice shows the groom charged the session to a corporate card that isn't his
23. **The Chandelier Silence**: Room goes dead quiet after a whisper → bride says the groom's father is the reason her sister disappeared
24. **The Phone Rings Once**: After the last reveal, one phone rings in the back → groom turns as if he already knows who it is
25. **The Mother Stands**: Groom's mother rises with a smile → announces she owns the venue and is canceling the reception now
