# Nano Banana 2 — Cinematic Drama Photo Reference

Professional AI image prompts for **4:5 vertical cinematic drama stills** — the ReelShort / viral Facebook soap-opera aesthetic (see `sources.txt` sample images).

---

## Platform specs

| Dimension | Spec |
|-----------|------|
| Model | **Nano Banana 2** (Google) |
| Aspect ratio | **4:5 vertical** (every prompt) |
| Language | **English** |
| Setting | United States — specific city/region + time + lighting |
| Use | Facebook hero image → long caption → full story article |

---

## Visual style — Cinematic drama still

Every prompt must read like a **high-budget soap opera / short-drama key frame**, not a casual phone snapshot.

### Required cues (include 4–6 per prompt)

| Criterion | Write like this |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Style | cinematic photorealistic drama still, high-budget soap opera aesthetic, ReelShort key frame |
| Lighting | soft Rembrandt side light, natural daylight with gentle contrast, or warm interior chandelier glow |
| Framing | medium-wide or medium shot, shallow depth of field, poster-like composition |
| Faces | emotionally charged expressions readable at scroll size — shock, grief, defiance, tenderness |
| Wardrobe | deliberate color coding (see below) |
| Setting | specific US location visible — hospital entrance, airport arrivals, mountain porch, ballroom |
| Texture | realistic skin, fabric detail, cinematic polish — not plastic AI gloss |

### Color coding (character roles)

| Color | Role signal |
|-------|-------------|
| **White / cream / lace** | Protagonist purity, bride, mother |
| **Red (dress, scrubs, velvet suit)** | Power, shock, rival, emotional peak |
| **Emerald / navy / charcoal suit** | Professional protagonist or corporate antagonist |
| **Silver / sequin evening** | Glamorous antagonist (mother-in-law, "real mom") |
| **Dirty / barefoot / bandage** | Vulnerability, children in peril |

### Forbidden aesthetic cues

```
✗ smartphone snapshot, iPhone candid, phone-camera grain
✗ flat overhead fluorescent only (unless hospital scene)
✗ cartoon, illustration, anime
✗ watermark, logo, readable subtitles
✗ graphic violence, sexual content, minors in danger posed sexually
```

---

## Title format

```
NNN | [tension phrase] → [payoff phrase]
```

Title names full arc for production. **Photo may show the scroll-stop emotional peak** — including partial twist visibility when it maximizes hook (airport kids running, doctor crying, under-bed tableau).

---

## Photo prompt structure

```
4:5 vertical cinematic photorealistic drama still, high-budget soap opera aesthetic, shallow depth of field, emotionally charged faces.

[US location + time + lighting]. [Character lock — age, hair, outfit for every person in frame].

The scene captures [scroll-stop moment]: [who is doing what, body language, expressions]. [Object or environmental clue — ring, cake, recording phone, stale bread, cardboard box]. [Background witnesses — guests, nurses, bodyguards, forest].

Framing: [medium-wide / low angle from floor / eye-level tableau]. Cinematic photorealism, natural skin texture, no readable text on signs, documents, phone screens, or cake icing — blur or angle away from legible words. No watermark.
```

## What the hero photo should show

| Beat | Show in photo? | Notes |
|------|----------------|-------|
| Core emotional hook | **Yes** | The moment that makes the reader stop scrolling |
| Antagonist + protagonist together | **Often** | Multi-character tableau is preferred |
| Partial twist visible | **Allowed** | e.g. boys running to mom, doctor crying — if caption still holds deeper payoff |
| Full legal/business resolution | **No** | Court judgment, stock transfer, eviction notice signed |
| Complete dialogue payoff | **No** | Mic-drop lines stay in caption/story |

### Composition patterns (from sample images)

| Pattern | Use when |
|---------|----------|
| **Split-level** | Hidden witness under bed / under table while others plot above |
| **Triangle of shock** | Joyful foreground action + frozen witnesses in background |
| **Kneeling protector** | Adult at child eye level — cottage twins, hospital child |
| **Exit walk** | Protagonist walking away from building, antagonist watching |
| **Medical breakdown** | Professional loses composure at bedside |

### Action-in-photo engines

For **E05 Crisis Hero**, **E07 Honest Return**, **E19 Good Deed Witnessed**, **E22 Underdog Wins** — show the peak *action* directly; outcome still hidden.

---

## Documents, screens & readable text

AI struggles with legible in-scene text. **Prefer faces and composition over readable words.**

| Instead of... | Write... |
|---------------|----------|
| Legible cake text | tall white cake with blue frosting message, words not readable |
| Airport sign "Arrivals" | yellow airport arrivals sign, text blurred |
| Hospital sign | glass doors with hospital entrance signage, text illegible |
| Phone screen content | smartphone with screen angled away, red recording dot visible |

---

## Policy safety

- Fictional characters only
- No graphic violence, gore, weapons pointed at people
- No sexual content; pregnancy shown modestly (gown, hand on stomach)
- Children: distressed or emotional OK; not sexualized, not in peril for shock only
- Infidelity/alluded — tension OK, no explicit scenes

See Phase 0 plan for edge cases.
