## C071 — The New Wife Sent the Maid to the Basement → The Maid Was the Owner's Sister, Recording Everything
**conceptSource:** concepts5 #F07-01 · **engine:** E04 Test in Disguise · **tone:** mixed · **openingStyle:** Quiet observation

- **Setup:** Worried about how her ailing brother's glamorous new wife treats his household, Renata goes undercover as a temporary housekeeper at his Charleston estate.
- **Clue:** The new wife, Sabrina, is gracious in front of guests but cruel to staff, and openly plots to sideline the brother's grown children once he's gone.
- **Turn:** When Sabrina banishes the "maid" to the basement during a dinner party and brags about her plans, Renata quietly records it — then removes her uniform cap and introduces herself.
- **Payoff (full story):** Renata's recordings protect her brother and his children; the test reveals exactly who Sabrina is before she can do real harm.

**Character Lock:**

- Sabrina Vance — 38, the new wife, sequined cocktail dress, champagne flute, pointing toward the basement door
- Dinner guests — well-dressed couples frozen at a long table, mid-toast
- Setting — grand antebellum-style estate dining room, Charleston, South Carolina, evening, chandelier light
- Object clue — a small phone tucked in the apron pocket of the "maid" (pays off in full story)

**Peak-tension photo moment:** Renata in the maid's uniform near the basement door as Sabrina gestures dismissively, guests frozen at the table — her true identity not yet revealed.
