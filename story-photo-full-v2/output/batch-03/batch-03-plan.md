### C021 | Eulogy Interruption → Brother Clears Throat in Back Pew

**Engine:** E11 (Bittersweet / Too Late)
**openingStyle:** Gut-punch question
**conceptSource:** concepts5 #F04-03

**4-beat arc:**
- **Setup:** Marcus delivers a tearful eulogy for his older brother Elijah at a historic funeral chapel in Charleston, family weeping.
- **Clue:** A man in a worn army jacket slips into the last pew; his hands grip a faded military dog tag.

- **Payoff:** Marcus embraces him but learns the creditors already seized the family restaurant two days ago — the sacrifice was too late.

**Character Lock:**
- Marcus Tate — 38, charcoal suit, shaved head, reading folded paper at lectern, tears on cheeks
- Elijah Tate — 42, gaunt, worn olive army jacket, dog tag chain visible at collar, standing in back pew
- Pastor Williams — 60s, white collar, open Bible, frozen mid-gesture near pulpit
- Setting — Laurel Hill Funeral Chapel, Charleston, South Carolina, arched stained glass, Saturday 2pm, overcast light through colored panels
- Object clue — faded military dog tag on silver chain (connects to faked deployment story)

**Peak-tension moment for photo (Beat 1–2):** Marcus mid-eulogy, tears streaming, while a shadowed figure in an army jacket sits in the very last pew — congregation does not see him yet.
