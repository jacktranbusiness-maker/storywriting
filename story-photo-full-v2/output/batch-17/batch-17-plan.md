### C161 — "Every Night the Old Man Bowled One Quiet Game in Mismatched Rental Shoes -> The Day He Saved the Place"
- **conceptSource:** #F06-20 | **engine:** E31 Provider in Disguise | **tone:** feel-good | **opening:** Quiet devotion

Clue: Sunset Lanes is failing; a foreclosure notice is taped to the door; the youth league is about to lose its home. Turn: at the closing-night meeting, Petrov reveals he quietly bought the alley to stop the foreclosure. Payoff: the league is saved; he asks only that the kids be kind to the next lonely regular; warm surprise.
- **Character Lock:** Dani Cruz (19, narrator, lane attendant, Sunset Lanes polo); Mr. Petrov (79, threadbare cardigan, mismatched red/blue rental shoes, bowls alone nightly); Tyler & friends (16, teen mockers); Coach Ramos (50s, youth league). Setting: Sunset Lanes, Davenport IA, evening, neon and worn lanes. Object clue: a foreclosure notice on the glass door.
- **Peak photo:** Petrov mid-release on a worn lane in mismatched rental shoes, teens smirking behind him; Dani watching from the counter; a foreclosure notice taped to the glass door in the background. Twist hidden.
