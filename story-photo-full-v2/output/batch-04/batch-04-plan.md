## C031 — The Jeweler Read the Engraving Out Loud → It Was Another Woman's Birthday
**conceptSource:** concepts5 #F03-10 · **engine:** E00 Humiliation → Reveal · **tone:** mixed · **openingStyle:** In-the-moment action

- **Setup:** Three days before her wedding, Holly stops at a Cincinnati jeweler with her fiancé Brett to have his ring resized and the inscription checked.
- **Clue:** The jeweler, reading the inside of the band aloud to confirm the order, recites a date that is not their wedding date and not Brett's birthday.
- **Turn:** Holly recognizes the date as the birthday of Brett's "just a coworker" Vanessa, and the inscription is a private love line.
- **Payoff (full story):** The ring was originally engraved for Vanessa; Holly calmly verifies it, calls off the wedding before more deposits clear, and exposes the affair to both families on her own terms.

**Character Lock:**
- Holly Marsh — 31, honey-blonde hair in soft waves, cream blouse, small engagement ring she keeps glancing at
- Brett Cole — 33, fiancé, navy quarter-zip, easy salesman smile going stiff
- Jeweler — 60s, half-moon glasses, loupe on a cord, holding the ring up to a counter lamp
- Setting — small jewelry shop, Cincinnati, Ohio, weekday afternoon, warm display-case lighting
- Object clue — the engraved wedding band catching the lamp light on a velvet pad (pays off in story)

**Peak-tension photo moment:** Holly's face freezing as the jeweler reads the inscription; Brett's smile stiffening — the date itself not shown.
