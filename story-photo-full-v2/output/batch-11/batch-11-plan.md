- **Payoff (full story):** Quinn reads the room and the screen in real time, calmly ends the marriage on her own terms before the cake, and keeps her dignity while the groom loses everything.

**Peak-tension photo moment:** Trey mid-toast with the mic as the big screen behind him begins to glow with a phone display; Quinn's champagne flute frozen halfway down — the messages not yet readable.
