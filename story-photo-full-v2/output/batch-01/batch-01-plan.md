## C001 — Smart-Speaker Confession → The Name She Wasn't Supposed to Hear
**conceptSource:** concepts5 #F05-01 · **engine:** E00 Humiliation → Reveal · **tone:** mixed · **openingStyle:** Overheard dialogue

- **Setup:** Dana Reyes, a hospital scheduler in Sacramento, asks her kitchen smart speaker to replay the morning news while her husband Marcus showers.
- **Clue:** Instead of the news, the device replays a routine it logged the night before — a calendar request to "book the lake cabin for me and Sloane," said in Marcus's voice at 1 a.m.
- **Turn:** Dana keeps her face still, exports the device's voice history, and matches the timestamps to Marcus's "late shifts."
- **Payoff (full story):** Sloane is Marcus's coworker and the cabin is in Dana's grandmother's name; Dana revokes the booking, surfaces the log to the family group, and Marcus's confident story collapses publicly.

**Character Lock:**
- Dana Reyes — 38, brown shoulder-length hair in a loose clip, gray hospital scrubs, wedding band she keeps turning
- Marcus Reyes — 41, husband, damp hair, white undershirt, phone face-down on the counter
- Smart speaker — cylinder device on the kitchen windowsill, soft glowing ring
- Setting — small kitchen, Sacramento, California, 6:50 a.m., flat morning light through blinds
- Object clue — a sticky note on the fridge reading a name partially covered by a magnet (pays off in story)

**Peak-tension photo moment:** Dana frozen at the counter, mug halfway down, eyes on the glowing speaker — the recording playing, payoff not shown.
