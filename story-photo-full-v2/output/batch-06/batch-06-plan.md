## 4-BEAT ARCS (Summary)

### C051 | Art Collection → Missing Master
- **Setup:** Heirs fight over cash/property; dismiss father's "worthless" paintings.
- **Clue:** Appraiser Hugo studies one ignored canvas; his gloves tremble.
- **Turn:** Hugo announces the painting is a missing Impressionist master worth $14M — more than the entire estate combined.
- **Payoff:** Father's will stipulates: paintings go to whichever heir "valued what he loved." Only Noelle never dismissed them. She inherits the collection.

### C052 | Zoom Record → Whistleblower
- **Setup:** VP Derek fires Janelle in a "confidential" Zoom meeting, citing performance.
- **Clue:** Janelle's account had auto-cloud-record enabled by IT policy; she didn't disable it.
- **Turn:** Recording captures Derek admitting the firing is retaliation for her internal safety report.
- **Payoff:** Janelle shares the link with the labor board; Derek is terminated, Janelle is reinstated with back pay.

### C053 | Doorbell Reunion → Court Order
- **Setup:** Rachel and David prepare adoption finalization for 7-year-old Marcus.
- **Clue:** Doorbell rings twenty minutes before leaving for the courthouse.
- **Turn:** Birth mother Tanya stands on the porch with a court order stopping the adoption — she completed rehab and parenting classes.
- **Payoff:** Bittersweet: Tanya's claim is legally valid; Marcus is returned. The Underwoods lose the child they raised for three years.

### C054 | Pipeline Easement → Water Rights
- **Setup:** Pipeline company threatens eminent domain; sends a bulldozer schedule.
- **Clue:** Clara's late husband left a filing cabinet she never opened; inside: water-rights deed.
- **Turn:** Clara's deed predates the pipeline easement map — the aquifer crossing is on her mineral estate.
- **Payoff:** Company withdraws; Clara negotiates a conservation easement that protects her land permanently.

### C055 | Shared Calendar → Affair
- **Setup:** Sophie opens the family calendar to add a school event; notices recurring "client dinners."
- **Clue:** All dinners map to the same address — an apartment building across town.
- **Turn:** Sophie tells her mother; together they confirm David's car GPS matches the address on dinner nights.
- **Payoff:** Rebecca confronts David calmly with printed evidence; files for divorce the following week.

### C056 | Smell of Bread → Bakery Chain
- **Setup:** Miriam, discharged after a car accident with retrograde amnesia, walks past a bakery.
- **Clue:** The smell triggers a flood — her hands remember kneading, her feet remember the tile floor.
- **Turn:** She walks in; manager Elena recognizes her and drops the bread tray. Miriam is the founder who vanished 14 months ago.
- **Payoff:** Miriam's memory returns over weeks; she resumes ownership with Elena as full partner.

### C057 | Grand Opening → Unpaid Wages Shirts
- **Setup:** Chef Anton toasts his new bistro in front of investors and press.
- **Clue:** A group in matching black t-shirts files through the entrance.
- **Turn:** The shirts list dollar amounts of unpaid wages; Luisa reads a statement to the crowd.
- **Payoff:** Investors pull funding; health department arrives next day over related violations; bistro closes within a month.

### C058 | Child's Voice Memo → Custody Reversal
- **Setup:** Frank seeks full custody; claims Angela is unstable. Judge leans his way.
- **Clue:** Nine-year-old Zoe sits silently with her pink backpack, overlooked.
- **Turn:** Zoe asks to speak; plays a voice memo from her phone — Frank threatening to "disappear" her if she talks.
- **Payoff:** Judge reverses preliminary ruling; Frank's custody is revoked; Angela gets full protection order.

### C059 | Building Campaign → Pastor Fraud
- **Setup:** Congregation pledges $180K for a new steeple; Pastor Cole handles contracts.
- **Clue:** Contractor James approaches Deacon Mae with his original bid — half what the church paid.
- **Turn:** Mae reads the true numbers during Sunday service; the difference went to Cole's personal account.
- **Payoff:** Cole confesses publicly; congregation votes to forgive but remove him. Mae leads the rebuild with transparent books.

### C060 | Family Business Chair → Proxy Votes
- **Setup:** Desmond votes sister Nadine off the board, claiming majority.
- **Clue:** Nadine arrives late with a sealed envelope from their hospitalized father.
- **Turn:** Envelope contains signed proxy votes — father gave Nadine his controlling shares after she cared for him during chemo.
- **Payoff:** Nadine has majority. Instead of retaliating, she offers Desmond a co-leadership role. Grace over revenge.
