## 4-BEAT ARCS (Summary)

### C151 | Searching Woman → Birth Mother (E08)
- **Setup:** Ruth shows up at a community-center reunion group; the Calloway siblings mock the older woman clutching a folder.
- **Clue:** A DNA results envelope pokes from her folder.
- **Turn:** The test proves Ruth is the biological mother the siblings were given up by decades ago.
- **Payoff:** Warm reunion; the mockers must reckon with the mother they nearly turned away.

### C152 | Nanny Cam → Undercover Reveal (E04)
- **Setup:** The Calloways install a hidden camera, certain the nanny is the problem.
- **Clue:** A badge wallet edge in the nanny's pocket.
- **Turn:** The footage instead documents their own neglect/medication misuse; Priya is a licensed social worker on assignment.
- **Payoff:** The test in disguise flips — the watchers are the watched; the children are protected.

### C153 | Retreat Guest → Affair Exposed (E00)
- **Setup:** Greg insists a "retreat leader" just needs a place to stay.
- **Clue:** A hotel sticker on Sienna's suitcase; a luggage tag in Lauren's hand.
- **Turn:** The sticker matches a hotel charge on Greg's card from a "work trip" — proof of the affair.
- **Payoff:** Lauren exposes it calmly and leaves on her own terms; humiliation reversed.

### C154 | Child's Drawing → Safe House (E11)
- **Setup:** A custody evaluation; Emma is asked to draw her family.
- **Clue:** She draws only one house.
- **Turn:** The therapist reveals the single house is the home with her mother — the only place Emma feels safe.
- **Payoff:** Bittersweet — the truth protects Emma, but the wound of a fractured family and a frightening father lingers.

### C155 | Career Mocked → Forced to Hire Her (E30)
- **Setup:** At the will reading, Chidi laughs at sister Adaeze's "little career."
- **Clue:** Adaeze's leather portfolio.
- **Turn:** The trust requires Chidi to employ Adaeze as CFO — only she can release the company's funds.
- **Payoff:** Role reversal met with grace; Adaeze leads without vengeance, and the family business is saved.

### C156 | Dust Field → New Formation (E06)
- **Setup:** Neighbors mock the widow Della's worthless dry acreage.
- **Clue:** A rolled geology survey map.
- **Turn:** A young geologist confirms a rich new formation runs under Della's fence line — she was right to hold the land.
- **Payoff:** Quiet vindication; Della's patience and instinct pay off, and the mockers eat their words.

### C157 | Wrong Twin → Identified by Gait (E12)
- **Setup:** The guilty twin sends his identical brother to take the fall in the defendant's chair.
- **Clue:** The defendant's walk.
- **Turn:** The victim, watching how the man moves, identifies that this isn't the culprit — the real twin has a distinct limp.
- **Payoff:** The swap collapses; the guilty twin's scheme becomes its own comeuppance.

### C158 | Quick Wedding → Living First Marriage (E11)
- **Setup:** Mariana and Andrei climb the courthouse steps to marry.
- **Clue:** A document folder in a waiting woman's hands.
- **Turn:** Oksana, Andrei's first wife, reveals their marriage was never legally dissolved — Mariana's wedding would be bigamy.
- **Payoff:** Bittersweet — Mariana is spared a fraudulent marriage, but love is exposed as a lie at the altar.

### C159 | Paid Speaker → Fraud Recognized (E12)
- **Setup:** A polished anti-bullying speaker collects a huge fee at a school assembly.
- **Clue:** A parent stands with her phone.
- **Turn:** Renata recognizes him from a public fraud conviction and shows the record.
- **Payoff:** The grifter is exposed; the school's money and trust are protected.

### C160 | Mocked in the Wheelchair → Hospital Founder (E31)
- **Setup:** Teens film and mock a slow elderly man in a wheelchair in a hospital lobby.
- **Clue:** A large donor plaque on the wall behind him.
- **Turn:** Harold founded and funded the very hospital wing they're standing in.
- **Payoff:** The provider in disguise responds with grace, turning the moment into a lesson rather than revenge.
