## 4-BEAT ARCS (Summary)

### C091 | Ancestry Kit → Only Related to the Insulted Guest (E08)
- **Setup:** At the Hartwell reunion, the family does cheap DNA kits as a party game and mocks Daniel, a quiet guest who brought a casserole.
- **Clue:** Two result cards on the table; one face-down.
- **Turn:** The results show the Hartwells aren't biologically related to each other the way they assumed — and Daniel is the only true blood match (a child given up decades ago by Meemaw).
- **Payoff:** Reunion. Meemaw recognizes her long-lost grandson; the family that mocked him must reckon with who he really is. Warm, not vengeful.

### C092 | Library Shush → Hidden Donor (E31)
- **Setup:** Walter, in a worn coat, reads in the new wing; the head librarian shushes and tries to move him along as if he doesn't belong.
- **Clue:** The donor plaque on the wall behind them.
- **Turn:** Walter is the anonymous benefactor who funded the entire wing.
- **Payoff:** Quiet warmth — he doesn't seek revenge; he funds a literacy program and asks that no one be made to feel unwelcome again.

### C093 | Kids' Table → Mortgage Holder (E00)
- **Setup:** In-laws relegate Amara to a folding card table at Thanksgiving to humiliate her.
- **Clue:** Folded legal document by her plate.
- **Turn:** Amara has quietly held the mortgage on the family home for two years after bailing them out.
- **Payoff:** She reveals it calmly; the power in the room inverts. She chooses dignity over cruelty.

### C094 | Birth Plan Override → Surrogate Is Biological Mother (E11)
- **Setup:** Intended parents dictate the birth plan; the surrogate is treated as a vessel.
- **Clue:** Medical POA folder on her tray.
- **Turn:** A clinic error means the surrogate is the biological mother; her POA gives her legal standing.
- **Payoff:** Bittersweet — the intended parents' dream collapses; the truth is undeniable but no one wins cleanly.

### C095 | GitHub Lockout → Authorship Proof (E13)
- **Setup:** CEO Brandon locks co-founder Mei Lin out and claims she contributed nothing.
- **Clue:** The commit-history graph on her laptop.
- **Turn:** The repository's authorship timeline proves she wrote the core algorithm.
- **Payoff:** The lone voice is vindicated before the investor; her equity and authorship are restored.

### C096 | Scholarship Snub → Winning Putt (E22, action-in-photo)
- **Setup:** Legacy kids exclude scholarship golfer Eli from the club's social world.
- **Clue:** His worn, taped putter against their premium clubs.
- **Turn:** Eli sinks the winning putt on 18 to take the club championship.
- **Payoff:** Vindication by performance; the snobs must hand him the trophy. Merit wins.

### C097 | Twin Swap → The Scar (E04)
- **Setup:** A twin attends a reunion impersonating her more-accomplished sister.
- **Clue:** The name tag with the wrong name; a smooth eyebrow where a scar should be.
- **Turn:** The yearbook editor (who once stitched that scar story into the yearbook) realizes the impostor lacks the scar.
- **Payoff:** The disguise is exposed; the truth about why she did it surfaces — a test of identity and worth.

### C098 | Support Group → The Bar Photo (E12)
- **Setup:** Holly tearfully shares her loneliness during her husband's "deployment."
- **Clue:** Renee's phone with a recent local bar photo.
- **Turn:** Renee recognizes Holly's "deployed" husband — he never left; he's been living a second life locally.
- **Payoff:** The lie collapses; comeuppance for the husband, solidarity among the wronged spouses.

### C099 | Donation Mocked → The Audit (E17)
- **Setup:** PTA treasurer Bethany publicly mocks Priya's small donation.
- **Clue:** Priya's tote bag with a binder of financial printouts.
- **Turn:** Priya, a forensic auditor, opens the books and exposes Bethany's skimming.
- **Payoff:** The community rallies — parents unite, funds are restored, the school is protected. Warm collective justice.

### C100 | Live-Stream Comment → Second Marriage (E11)
- **Setup:** A picture-perfect vineyard wedding is being live-streamed to thousands.
- **Clue:** The scrolling comment feed on the tripod phone.
- **Turn:** A comment from the officiant's wife uploads proof he's secretly married — exposing fraud at the altar.
- **Payoff:** Bittersweet — the ceremony shatters publicly, but the bride is saved from a deeper deception; grace amid the wreckage.
