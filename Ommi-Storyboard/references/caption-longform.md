# Long-Form Facebook Caption Reference

**One caption per full story** (30–40s), not one per act.

---

## Specs

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Length | 1000–1200 characters |
| Language | English |
| Structure | 3 paragraphs + blank line + CTA |
| CTA | Only `MORE`, `YES`, or `NEXT` |
| Dialogue | Quote verbatim from **video prompts only** |
| Ending | Begin twist in paragraph 3 — **do not complete Act 4 payoff** |

---

## Paragraph roles

**Paragraph 1 — Hook:** Open with strongest insult or unfair moment (often Act 1 dialogue). Establish who is wronged and how fast.

**Paragraph 2 — Pressure:** Deepen humiliation. One specific visual from Act 2 (ring, clenched hands, torn backpack). Optional one quiet dialogue quote.

**Paragraph 3 — Shift:** Something changes — entrance, a line of recognition beginning, object catching light. Stop one step before full reversal.

**CTA:** Blank line, then e.g. `Drop 'MORE' if you want to know what happens next!! ⤵️`

---

## Opening styles (rotate if batch)

`Dialogue slap` · `Silent witness` · `Object clue` · `Accuser POV` · `Victim restraint` ·
`Room goes wrong` · `Visual reveal` · `Moral choice`

---

## Anti-patterns

- Do not summarize all 4 acts
- Do not reveal Act 4 twist ("she was Marcus's daughter all along")
- Do not invent dialogue not in video prompts
- Do not use "everyone froze" as closing line

---

## Allowed CTA formats

```
Drop 'MORE' if you want to know what happens next!! ⤵️
Comment 'YES' if you want the full story!! 👉
Type 'NEXT' if you want the next part!! ⬇️
```
